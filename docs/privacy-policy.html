Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Osprey Technology ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Osprey Smart Home mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Summary: We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the App. We do not sell your data, do not show ads, and do not use tracking services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • First and last name (optional, for profile personalization)

Device and Home Data:

  • Smart home device identifiers (MAC addresses, device names)
  • Home and room configurations you create
  • Automation schedules and scene settings
  • Device control commands and preferences

Technical Data:

  • Authentication tokens (stored securely on your device)
  • App settings and preferences (theme, language, temperature unit)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data (Bluetooth permission is used solely for device scanning)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Health, financial, or biometric data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account management: To create and maintain your account
  • Device control: To communicate with and manage your smart home devices
  • Automation: To execute your scheduled scenes and automations
  • Voice assistant integration: To link with Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant when you choose to do so

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your credentials are stored using encrypted platform keystores on your device
  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS
  • We do not store passwords in plain text
  • Authentication uses industry-standard JWT tokens

5. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with the following third-party services at your request:

  • Amazon Alexa: For voice control of your devices (subject to Amazon's privacy policy)
  • Google Assistant: For voice control of your devices (subject to Google's privacy policy)

We do not share your personal data with any third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data will be permanently removed from our servers

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Permissions

  • Bluetooth: Required to discover and connect to smart home devices
  • Location: Required by Android for Bluetooth device scanning (we do not track or store your location)
  • Internet: Required to communicate with the smart home server

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

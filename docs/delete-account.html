Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your Osprey Smart Home account and all associated data.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including device configurations, automations, and scenes will be permanently removed.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (email, name)
  • All home and room configurations
  • All device settings and pairings
  • All automations and scenes
  • Voice assistant connections (Alexa, Google Assistant)

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@ospreytech.io
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Contact

Email: support@ospreytech.io

Processing time: Up to 30 days
